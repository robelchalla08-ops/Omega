The IRL → ERL cycle

When external liquidity (ERL) is taken, the next DOL is internal — an FVG or equal H/L. When internal (IRL) is taken, the next DOL is external — a swing high/low. They alternate. Knowing where you are in this cycle tells you whether an Omega zone is a reversal point or a pit stop.

Applied to Omega: If Asia high was swept (external BSL taken), the LG:0 midpoint zone is now the primary internal DOL. After LG:0 is touched (internal taken), price targets the PDH above (external). Map which cycle you're in before entering.
